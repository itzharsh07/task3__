Discover Darbhanga

Darbhanga, located in the northern part of Bihar, is a city steeped in history and culture. Known for its association with the Mithila region, it serves as a vibrant hub of Maithili language, art, and traditions.

One of the city's most iconic landmarks is the Darbhanga Palace, an architectural marvel that reflects the grandeur of the former royal family. The Raj Darbhanga was one of the most influential zamindari estates during British rule in India. The palace and its surroundings are a popular attraction for tourists and history enthusiasts.

Darbhanga is also a center for classical music and education. The city has given rise to the Darbhanga Gharana, known for its unique style of dhrupad singing. In addition, the Lalit Narayan Mithila University stands as a beacon of higher education in the region.
